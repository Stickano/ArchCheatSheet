**# A list of some nice additions**

== Record My Desktop ==
**# **//gtk-recordmydesktop//** allows you to record videos of your dekstop**
$ pacman -S gtk-recordmydesktop


== System info and distro logo in terminal ==
**# **//alsi//** will output systeminfo along with a distro logo in the terminal**
$ yaourt -S alsi


== Transparency for terminals and conky etc ==
**# NOTE that dekstop environments typical have their own composite manager**
**# **//xcompmgr //**is a composite Window-effect manager **
$ pacman -S xcompmgr

**# Add it to some autostart script **
	(xcompmgr -c) &
