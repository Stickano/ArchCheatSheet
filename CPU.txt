== Thermald ==
**# Will help keep your system at a accepted temperature**
$ yaourt -S thermald

== i7z ==
**# Reporting tool for Intel processors**
$ pacman -S i7z

**# Invoke with **
$ i7z

== CpuPower ==
**# Assists with CPU frequency scaling** 
$ pacman -S cpupower

**#** **Enable the service**
$ systemctl enable cpupower.service
