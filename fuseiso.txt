====== fuseiso ======
Created s√∏ndag 04 december 2016

**# Fuseiso will allow you to mount .iso images. **

== Installation ==
$ yaourt -S fuseiso

== Usage ==
**# To mount a image file**
$ fuseiso -p //<image>//.iso ///path/to/mountpoint//

**# To unmount a image file**
$ fusermount -u ///path/to/mountpoint//
